COMPUTER DEPARTMENT SVNIT SURAT
  1. The undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering at S V National Institute of Technology, has been offered
        since 1988-89, with the first batch of Bachelors of Computer Engineering graduating in 1992 (intake 30)
        under the patronage of the erstwhile Department of Electronics Engineering.
        The Department of Computer Engineering was bifurcated from the Department of Electronics Engineering
        in 2002. With a focus on balancing high-quality research to substantiate the class-room teaching the Ph D
       program has been started in the year 2005 and the inception of the M Tech course was taken place in 2006.
        The department has also started the PG progamme, M.Tech. (by Research) in the year 2007.
        Currently, it offers the UG, PG and PhD degrees in Computer Engineering.
